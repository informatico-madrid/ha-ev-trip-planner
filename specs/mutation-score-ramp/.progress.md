#### Classification: Stronger Test / US-5 (b) — ~58 of 743 survivors

A smaller subset of survivors could potentially be killed with stronger tests. These fall into two categories:

1. **String literal mutations in field mapping tuples** (~25 survivors): e.g., `("dia_semana", "dia_semana") → ("dia_semana", "XXdia_semanaXX")` in make_trip_update_handler. To kill these, tests would need to verify that ONLY the exact expected field names are used in the update dict, which is fragile and implementation-specific.

2. **`or False`/`or True` mutations in conditional chains** (~33 survivors): e.g., `if coordinator:` → `if coordinator or True:` or `if not coordinator:` → `if not coordinator or False:`. Killing these would require verifying that `async_refresh_trips` is called/not-called, but this is a side-effect verification that is very fragile with HA mocks.

#### US-5 Assessment

The services module is fundamentally different from utils/sensors:
